R A H W A N A TRISULA

Rahwana Trisula studio is a Jakarta - based collaborative practice established studio, that aims to investigate the boundaries between art, technology and future. Rahwana's work focuses on experience design, visual graphics, motion, animation, films, digital, structures of art installation and programs to new and imaginative immersive effect. With a conceptual approach centered on the importance of experience, that creates unexpected and memorable effects. By transforming the familiar into the extraordinary.
