88 Rising Rich Brian Concert

On 2 June 2020, Rich Brian and 88Rising squad did a concert in JIExpo Arena, The concert that themed "Head in the Clouds Jakarta" is also performed by other artists that is NIKI, Joji, and also other famous artists. This concert is packed by the 25.000 viewers that came from all around the world.

Who is Rich Brian? Brian Imanuel Soewarno (born 3 September 1999), known professionally as Rich Brian, is an Indonesian rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his viral debut single "Dat $tick", which was first released in March 2016 on SoundCloud. The single was later certified gold by RIAA. His debut studio album, Amen, was released in February 2018, and peaked at number 18 on the US Billboard 200. Brian's second studio album, The Sailor, was released on 26 July 2019
